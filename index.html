Our Process

Farm to Cup
Journey

Step 01

Farm Partnership

We visit and partner directly with sustainable farms across 25+ countries, ensuring fair wages and eco-conscious practices.

Step 02

Careful Harvesting

Hand-picked at peak ripeness, only the finest cherries make the journey to become Ember & Bean coffee.

Step 03

Processing & Sorting

Each batch is carefully processed, dried, and sorted to ensure only flawless green beans proceed to roasting.

Step 04

Artisan Roasting

Our master roasters craft each profile to unlock the bean's highest potential — never over-roasted, never rushed.

Step 05

Your Perfect Cup

From roaster to your hands within 48 hours, ensuring the freshest, most vibrant coffee experience possible.
